Loan-Policy-Rules:

1. Minimum Salary Requirement:
   The applicant must have a minimum monthly in-hand salary of â‚¹25,000.

2. CIBIL/Credit Score Criteria:
   - A minimum Credit Score of 700 is required for loan eligibility.
   - Scores above 800 are considered excellent and may qualify for lower interest rates.

3. Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio:
   The maximum allowed Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratio is 55%. This means the sum of all existing EMIs plus the proposed new EMI should not exceed 55% of the monthly salary.

4. Interest Rate Determination:
   - Base Interest Rate: 13% per annum.
   - For Credit Score > 750: 11% per annum.
   - For Credit Score > 800: 9% per annum.

5. Maximum Loan Amount Eligibility:
   The maximum loan amount sanctioned is capped at 20 times the applicant's monthly salary, subject to DTI constraints.

6. Loan Tenure:
   - Minimum Tenure: 1 year.
   - Maximum Tenure: 5 years (60 months).
   - Tenure cannot exceed the retirement age of the applicant (assumed 60 years).

7. Employment Type:
   Loans are primarily available for Salaried individuals with at least 1 year of continuous employment. Self-employed individuals require 2 years of business continuity proof (ITR).

8. Foreclosure and Prepayment:
   - No foreclosure charges after 12 EMIs have been paid.
   - Prepayment allowed up to 25% of principal outstanding once a year without penalty.
